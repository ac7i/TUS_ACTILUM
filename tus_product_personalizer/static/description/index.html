Setup & Configuration

Install Odoo Product Personalizer
From App Menu > Install Module

1. Module Configuration

Go to: Sales → Products → Products, and open the product you want to configure. Once installed, navigate to the Product Designer Settings (visible as the Print To Web tab) to enable product editing features.go to the "Print to Web" tab. Click "Add a line".

2. Add Product Configuration:

Here define your product’s size specifications such as Height, Width, and other relevant settings. You can also set the design area and upload images as needed.

3. Add Product Designs to Your Item

Go to Sales → Products → Products. Then, click on the Print to web tab. Under this tab, you can upload and manage product design templates, set the design area, and configure size settings. These templates act as base designs that users can customize using the product designer.

4. Variant-Based Product Design Image Configuration

Go to Sales → Products → Product Variants and select a product (e.g., T-shirt). Choose the required variant, and the design image will change accordingly. Then open the Product Designs tab to upload or manage design images for each variant.

5. Product Design Customization

On the product page (e.g., T-shirt), click the "Customize Design" button. This opens the product editor where users can visually customize the design - including modifying layout, images, and text - before adding the item to their cart.

6. Swap Product & Customize Design

On swapping the product, designs can be added or modified for any product variant, allowing flexible customization across different variants.

7. Add Image to Product Design

Using the Add Image option, images can be uploaded and placed directly on the T-shirt within the defined design area. These images will be included in the final printed design of the product. Text and shapes can also be added, and layers can be managed to control the final printed design of the product.

8. Save Design as Template

On clicking Save Logo, the product design is automatically saved as a template. All saved templates can be viewed and reused for future product designs.

9. Preview & Download Customized Design

On clicking Preview Logo, the customized product design can be viewed in detail. The design can be downloaded using the Download button from the preview screen.

10. Sales Order Design Preview

sales manager can view the customized design by clicking the “View Design” button directly in the sales order line.

Can I customize business card designs using the Product Designer?

Yes! The Product Designer allows you to customize business card designs by adding logos, text, colors, and other elements to match your brand identity.

Does the Product Designer support different product types like T-shirts, mugs, and business cards?

Yes! The module can handle multiple product types, allowing users to create designs for business cards, T-shirts, mugs, and more.

Can I use Google Fonts for text in the product designer?

Yes, you can use Google Fonts in the product designer! However, you need to configure your Google Fonts API key in the General Settings to enable this feature.
